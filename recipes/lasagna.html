Lasagna

What is Lasagna?

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's baked to perfection, resulting in a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

How to make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

Once you have all your ingredients, you're ready to start making lasagna. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the meat, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, sugar, and spices to the meat mixture. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and more meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
