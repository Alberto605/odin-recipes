Beef Miso Ramen

What Is Beef Miso Ramen?

This Beef Miso Ramen recipe features tender beef slices, chewy ramen noodles, and a rich miso broth, topped with fresh vegetables and a soft-boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • 200g beef slices
  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup chopped bok choy
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs (optional)

How to Make Beef Miso Ramen

Making Beef Miso Ramen is a straightforward process. Follow these steps for a delicious meal:

  1. In a large pot, heat the sesame oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and saut√© until fragrant.
  2. Add the beef slices and cook until browned. Remove the beef and set aside.
  3. Pour in the beef broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the miso paste and soy sauce until well combined.
  4. Add the sliced mushrooms and chopped bok choy to the broth. Simmer for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Meanwhile, cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. To assemble, divide the cooked noodles into bowls. Ladle the hot miso broth over the noodles, ensuring each bowl gets a good amount of vegetables and beef slices.
  7. Top with chopped green onions and a soft-boiled egg if desired.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Beef Miso Ramen!
